# Project Brief

## Recommended Title

**从对话到编队：面向多智能体协作的指挥式界面研究**

This title avoids the harder phrase “交互抽象” and makes the project easier to explain. The key phrase is **指挥式界面**: the user is not merely chatting with AI, but commanding a team of agents.

## Alternative Titles

1. **从提示到编队：面向多智能体系统的指挥式交互研究**
2. **从聊天助手到代理小队：多智能体协作中的编队式界面研究**
3. **面向多智能体协作的编队式人机交互研究**
4. **Agent 时代的指挥式界面：多智能体编队、态势理解与人类接管研究**

## Plain-Language Explanation

“交互抽象” sounds too academic and hard to understand. In this project, the simpler idea is:

> We need a new way for humans to control a team of AI agents.

Today, users usually either talk to one AI assistant or watch several role-based agents chat. This project proposes a more understandable control method: **formation**.

A formation means a predefined collaboration posture for a group of agents. For example:

- **Scouting formation**: agents explore different directions in parallel.
- **Debate formation**: agents argue from different positions before a conclusion is formed.
- **Execution formation**: agents plan, execute, and monitor a task step by step.
- **Audit formation**: agents check evidence, risk, mistakes, and missing assumptions.

## One-Sentence Thesis

Future users may not operate every software step themselves, nor rely on one all-powerful assistant; instead, they may command a flexible team of agents through readable formations.

## Research Positioning

This is an HCI and interaction design project. It does not aim to invent a stronger algorithm. It studies how users understand, command, and intervene in multi-agent collaboration.
