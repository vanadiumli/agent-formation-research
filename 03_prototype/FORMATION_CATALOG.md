# Formation Catalog

## Scouting Formation

Purpose: broad exploration.

Agent roles:

- Searcher: finds information and examples.
- Mapper: organizes directions.
- Skeptic: flags weak or unsupported directions.

Best for:

- Early topic exploration.
- Literature search.
- Finding design precedents.

## Debate Formation

Purpose: expose conflicts and alternatives.

Agent roles:

- Proponent: argues for a direction.
- Opponent: challenges assumptions.
- Judge: synthesizes trade-offs.

Best for:

- Topic selection.
- Design decision making.
- Comparing competing concepts.

## Execution Formation

Purpose: finish a concrete task.

Agent roles:

- Planner: breaks the task down.
- Executor: produces outputs.
- Watchdog: monitors risk and completion.

Best for:

- Drafting a proposal.
- Preparing a presentation.
- Producing structured deliverables.

## Audit Formation

Purpose: verify quality and risk.

Agent roles:

- Evidence checker: checks source support.
- Gap finder: identifies missing pieces.
- Reviewer: evaluates clarity and contribution.

Best for:

- Pre-submission review.
- Literature verification.
- Thesis defense preparation.
